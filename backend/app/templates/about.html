About Tranquil Tides

Tranquil Tides is a personalized mental wellness companion designed to help users assess and track their mental health. By integrating machine learning models and relaxation techniques, we aim to provide insights and resources that promote mental well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make mental health support more accessible and personalized through innovative technology.

Key Features

  • Self-Assessment Tools
  • Mood Tracking
  • Relaxation Techniques
  • Personalized Insights

Our Team

Tranquil Tides was developed by a team of dedicated mental health and technology enthusiasts committed to improving lives through innovation.
